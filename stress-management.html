Learn to Manage Stress

Stress is a part of life, but managing it effectively is essential for mental health. Techniques like mindfulness, deep breathing, and time-outs can help you stay calm under pressure.

Explore tips and exercises to reduce stress in your daily life:

  • Practice deep breathing exercises.
  • Take regular breaks to reset your focus.
  • Engage in relaxing hobbies like reading or drawing.

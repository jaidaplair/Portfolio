Master Your Time

Effective time management helps balance academic and personal commitments. Learn to structure your day for maximum productivity.

Here are some tips to manage your time better:

  • Create a daily schedule and prioritize tasks.
  • Avoid multitasking; focus on one task at a time.
  • Use tools like calendars and to-do lists to stay organized.
